# NDUFB7_Mito_2026 --- AI提示词 V50 FINAL（2026-04-24 07:00）

## 核心叙事（投稿级）
"NDUFB7, a 137-aa microenvironment-sensitive accessory subunit of mitochondrial Complex I, 
exhibits fibrotic-zone-specific depletion in human myocardial infarction (Visium: FZ 40.2% 
vs IZ 64.9%, p<0.0001), contrasting with preservation in acute infarct zones. Multi-platform 
bulk validation across four independent cohorts (Affymetrix n=330, RNA-seq n=101) reveals 
no overall heart failure downregulation but an etiology-specific gradient: DCM shows relative 
preservation compared to ischemic cardiomyopathy (GSE57338: DCM>ICM, p=0.046; GSE116250: 
DCM≈ICM>NF, KW p=0.021). The discrepancy between RNA-seq RPKM (significant upregulation) 
and Affymetrix microarray data (no change) highlights platform-specific quantification biases 
for short genes, underscoring the importance of protein-level validation and spatial resolution."

整合结论:
1. Affymetrix双数据集一致: 无显著总体HF差异
2. RNA-seq count_data: DCM > Ischemic (p=0.046)  
3. RNA-seq RPKM: 显著上调，但受137aa长度偏倚影响，可信度较低
4. 最可信模式: "DCM相对保留/Ischemic中性" + "纤维化区空间丢失"
5. 蛋白组(PXD010154)和空间(Kuppe)是独立于转录组的金标准

## 错误库（E48-E56）

E56: RPKM长度标准化偏倚——NDUFB7仅137aa，RPKM = reads/(length/1000)高估短基因
解决: 使用TPM替代RPKM；与蛋白组iBAQ对比；报告时标注"RPKM-based quantification"
