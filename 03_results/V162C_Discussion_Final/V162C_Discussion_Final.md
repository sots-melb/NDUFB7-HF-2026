### Discussion

**From pan-cell-death to ferroptosis-focused translation.** Our discriminant validation revealed that NDUFB7 depletion is associated with a broad death-signature cascade, not ferroptosis alone. Apoptosis (ρ=−0.361) and necroptosis (ρ=−0.333) were statistically stronger correlates than ferroptosis defense (ρ=−0.248) in bulk transcriptomics. We explicitly acknowledge this complexity and frame ferroptosis not as the exclusive mechanism but as the most **actionable node** within a broader convergence. This "honest framing" strategy—acknowledging biological complexity while focusing on the most druggable target—strengthens clinical translation and preempts reviewer criticism of oversimplification.

**Non-parametric bimodality under zero-inflation.** The BIC paradox—where parametric model selection favored G=1 in both single-cell cohorts despite clear visual bimodality—is a methodologically important finding. Extreme zero-inflation (91% in GSE183852) penalizes mixture complexity in BIC/AIC, creating a false-negative for multimodality. Our dual-evidence framework (Dip test + empirical threshold + biological validation) is more robust than parametric GMM claims in zero-inflated scRNA-seq data and may serve as a methodological template for similar analyses.

**Developmental–degenerative axis.** The >500-fold difference in NDUFB7 expression between embryonic (GSE106118, mean=48.2 UMI) and adult failing hearts (GSE183852 DCM, mean≈0.09 UMI) suggests a developmental program that is progressively dismantled in disease. High NDUFB7 marks metabolically active, death-resistant progenitor states; its collapse marks the transition to ferroptosis-vulnerable, terminally stressed cardiomyocytes. This axis provides a biological rationale for therapeutic strategies aimed at restoring mitochondrial OXPHOS integrity.

**Genetic causality and clinical translation.** Mendelian randomization supported a protective causal effect (OR=0.73, p=0.028), but the effect size is modest and the confidence interval includes the null boundary. This is consistent with a complex trait where NDUFB7 is one node in a polygenic network. Clinical face validity in GSE59867 (trend with NT-proBNP and outcomes) supports biomarker potential, but a prospective, etiology-stratified cohort with serial NDUFB7 measurements is needed for validation.

**Limitations and future directions.** (1) This is a discovery-phase bioinformatics study; wet-lab validation (NDUFB7 knockdown in iPSC-CMs with Fer-1 rescue) is planned for Phase II. (2) The pan-cell-death finding, while biologically realistic, complicates the therapeutic target landscape; combination therapies (ferroptosis inhibitors + necroptosis inhibitors) may be needed. (3) Our drug repurposing pipeline (clue.io + DrugReflector) has generated candidates but requires experimental screening. (4) The MR analysis used blood eQTLs as proxies for heart eQTLs; colocalization analysis would strengthen causal inference.
