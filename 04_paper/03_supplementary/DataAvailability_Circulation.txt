DATA AVAILABILITY STATEMENT
(Circulation / Cardiovascular Research format)

The data that support the findings of this study are openly available. 
Spatial transcriptomics: Zenodo 6578047 (Kuppe et al., Nature 2022). 
Bulk transcriptomics: NCBI GEO GSE57338, GSE116250, GSE55296, GDS4772. 
Single-cell transcriptomics: NCBI GEO GSE183852. 
Proteomics: PRIDE PXD010154. 
Genetic data: eQTLGen Phase I and GTEx v11. 

Analysis scripts (R v4.3.1) are available at [GitHub repository link] and archived at Zenodo [DOI pending]. 

No custom code beyond publicly available R/Bioconductor packages was used. 
Package versions are documented in sessionInfo.txt (GitHub repository).
