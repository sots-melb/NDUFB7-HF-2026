DATA AVAILABILITY STATEMENT
(Nature Communications format)

All data supporting the findings of this study are available from public repositories. 
Spatial transcriptomics data were obtained from Zenodo (record 6578047, Kuppe et al., Nature 2022, https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.6578047). 
Bulk RNA-seq and microarray datasets were retrieved from NCBI Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO): GSE57338 (Affymetrix Human Gene 1.1 ST Array, n=313), GSE116250 (Illumina HiSeq 2000 RNA-seq RPKM, n=64), GSE55296 (Illumina HiSeq 2000 RNA-seq counts, n=37), and GDS4772 (Affymetrix U133A 2.0, n=17). 
Single-cell reference data were obtained from NCBI GEO (GSE183852, DCM nuclei, ~200,000 cells). 
Proteomics data were obtained from PRIDE (PXD010154, 19 fractions). 
Expression quantitative trait loci data were obtained from eQTLGen (Phase I, https://www.eqtlgen.org/phase1.html) and GTEx Portal (v11, https://gtexportal.org/). 

All derived data (normalized expression matrices, WGCNA module assignments, PROGENy pathway scores, and spatial coordinates) are available in the GitHub repository [link pending] and Zenodo archive [DOI pending] under MIT License. 

Source data for all figures and supplementary figures are provided with this paper.
